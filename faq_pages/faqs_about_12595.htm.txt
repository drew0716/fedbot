How can I request to have a Board member speak at an event?
Speaking requests for Board members should be emailed to
[emailÂ protected]
. We will respond as soon as we can.
To make the review process as efficient as possible, please include the following in your request:
which Board member your request is directed to;
a brief description of your organization (a link may be provided) and the purpose of the event;
whether the event is open to the public and the media; and
details of the proposed format:
Is the event virtual, in person, or hybrid?
Is the invitation for the Board member to participate in a panel discussion or give remarks?
Is there an entry fee to the event? If so, please disclose the price.
