College Fed Challenge is the team competition for students where schools compete to analyze current economic and financial conditions, and to formulate a monetary policy recommendation modeling the FOMC. It is intended to encourage students to learn more about the U.S. economy, the Federal Reserve System, and the implementation of monetary policy.
