Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice
Notice of a Meeting Closed to Public Observation pursuant to § 12 CFR 261b.7
On
Tuesday, January 21, 2025 at 11:00 a.m.
, a meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under procedures set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding 
                Public Observation of Meetings, 
                at the Board's offices at 20th Street and C Streets, N.W., Washington, D.C. and by audio/video conference call, to consider the following matters of official Board business.
Meeting Date:
Tuesday, January 21, 2025
Matter(s) Considered:
1.
Review and determination by the Board of Governors of the advance and discount rates to be charged by the Federal Reserve Banks.
