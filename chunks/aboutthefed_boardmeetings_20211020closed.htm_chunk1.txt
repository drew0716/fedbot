Closed Board Meeting on October 20, 2021 Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice Notice of a Meeting under Expedited Procedures On Wednesday, October 20, 2021 at 10:00 a.m. , a meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under expedited procedures, as set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding Public Observation of Meetings, via audio/video conference call, to consider the following matters of official Board business. Meeting Date: Wednesday, October 20, 2021 Matter(s) Considered: 1. Discussion of the Semiannual Financial Stability Report. 9(A)(i) Effective October 20, 2021 , the meeting was closed to public observation by Order of the Board of Governors 1 because the matters fall under exemption(s) 9(A)(i) of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. Section 552b(c)), and it was determined that the public interest did not require opening the meeting. For more information please contact: Michelle Smith, Director, Assistant to the Board, Division of Board Members at 202-452-2955.
