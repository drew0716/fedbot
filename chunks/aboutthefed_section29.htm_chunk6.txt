In Subsection (c). The maximum daily amount of any civil penalty which may be assessed pursuant to subsection (c) for any violation, practice, or breach described in such subsection is-- in the case of any person other than a member bank, an amount to not exceed $1,000,000; and in the case of a member bank, an amount not to exceed the lesser of -- $1,000,000; or 1 percent of the total assets of such member bank.
