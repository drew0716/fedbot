Information on open market operations includes the name of the counterparty, the CUSIP--a number similar to a serial number--of individual securities purchased or sold, and the price at which the operation was conducted. These transaction-level details on lending and on open market operations, released with a two-year delay as the law requires, supplement the aggregate information provided in weekly and quarterly reports.
