The Federal Reserve is the primary regulator of bank holding companies, state banks that are members of the Federal Reserve System, foreign banking organizations operating in the United States, and certain other types of financial companies. To determine whether the institution you're reporting about is regulated by the Federal Reserve, you can use the lookup tool provided by the Federal Financial Institutions Examination Council.
