Waivers Q79: To whom should a waiver request be sent? Updated: 4/15/2022 A79: Covered individuals who are Board employees must submit waiver requests to the Board’s Designated Agency Ethics Official. Covered individuals who are Reserve Bank employees must submit waiver requests to the Board's Designated Agency Ethics Official and their Reserve Bank Ethics Official. XXV. Reporting and disclosure Q80: Will covered individuals still be required to continue to comply with existing applicable annual financial disclosure reporting requirements under the Policy for Covered Individuals? Updated: 2/5/2024 A80: Yes. Q81: What does "promptly posted" mean in section 3(b) regarding the requirement to post any Periodic Transaction Reports and annual financial disclosure reports filed by a Reserve Bank president? Updated: 2/5/2024 A81: Consistent with the requirements for Board members’ financial disclosures, Reserve Bank presidents’ disclosures should be posted online within 30 days of submission of a signed report to the Reserve Bank ethics official.
