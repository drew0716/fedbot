PRIVACY ACT STATEMENT FOR APPLICATION FOR APPOINTMENT TO THE COMMUNITY ADVISORY COUNCIL ("CAC") PURPOSES AND USES This Privacy Act Statement is provided for individuals interested in appointment to the Community Advisory Council established by the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System ("Board"). The information you submit will be used to evaluate your qualifications, suitability, and availability for service on the CAC. The information may also be used to contact you concerning your interest in serving on the CAC.
