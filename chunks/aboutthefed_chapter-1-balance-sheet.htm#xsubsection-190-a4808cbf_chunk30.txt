At the close of business each December 31, this account balance should include only the low-value and infrequent SOMA-related transactions. 3.08 Federal Agency and GSE Mortgage-Backed Securities (145-300, 145-315, and 145-330) On January 5, 2009, the RMBS purchase program began to purchase RMBS guaranteed by Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and Ginnie Mae. On March 23, 2020, the CMBS purchase program began to purchase CMBS guaranteed by Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and Ginnie Mae. The securities are bought from or sold to securities dealers at market prices. By law, the securities must be fully guaranteed as to principal and interest by an agency of the United States. Outright transactions in RMBS and CMBS are recorded on the scheduled settlement date. (See paragraph 40.13 .) If a seller fails to provide a security to the FRBNY on the contractual settlement date, a fail liability is recorded. When securities are purchased or sold, the net amount of the transaction is paid to or collected by the FRBNY from the dealer
