Voluntary liquidation Any such corporation may go into voluntary liquidation and be closed by a vote of its shareholders owning two-thirds of its stock. [12 USC 623. As added by act of Dec. 24, 1919 (41 Stat. 382.)] 16. Appointment of receiver or conservator In General. The Board may appoint a conservator or receiver for a corporation organized under the provisions of this section to the same extent and in the same manner as the Comptroller of the Currency may appoint a conservator or receiver for a national bank, and the conservator or receiver for such corporation shall exercise the same powers, functions, and duties, subject to the same limitations, as a conservator or receiver for a national bank.
