Prior to the FAST Act, the Board levied an interest on Federal Reserve notes charge on the Reserve Banks. The FAST Act requires the transfer of excess earnings to Treasury. In both cases, the amount in Reserve Bank surplus is controlled by creating a non-discretionary obligation to transfer excess residual net earnings to Treasury.
