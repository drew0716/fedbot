Updated: 2/5/2024 A77: Section 2(h)(2)(iii) of the policy exempts "[u]ntaxed distributions from 529 plans, health savings accounts, or other similar accounts" from the one-year holding period, advance notice and pre-clearance process, and financial market stress period prohibitions. "Other similar accounts" is intended to cover tax-advantaged accounts that operate in a similar manner, such as an Achieving a Better Life Experience ("ABLE") account, which is a tax-advantaged account for individuals with disabilities and their families.
