Section 309 provides that the Ombuds: Is to act as a liaison between the agency and any affected person with respect to any problem such party may have in dealing with the agency resulting from the regulatory activities of the agency; and Is to assure that safeguards exist to encourage complainants to come forward and preserve confidentiality.
