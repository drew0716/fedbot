Closed Board Meeting on December 10, 2018 Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice Notice of a Meeting under Expedited Procedures On Monday, December 10, 2018 at 11:00 a.m. , a meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under expedited procedures, as set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding Public Observation of Meetings, at the Board's offices at 20th Street and C Streets, N.W., Washington, D.C., to consider the following matters of official Board business. Meeting Date: Monday, December 10, 2018 Matter(s) Considered: 1. Review and determination by the Board of Governors of the advance and discount rates to be charged by the Federal Reserve Banks. 9(A)(i) 2. Supervisory Matter. 8 Effective December 10, 2018 , the meeting was closed to public observation by Order of the Board of Governors 1 because the matters fall under exemption(s) 9(A)(i), 8 of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. Section 552b(c)), and it was determined that the public interest did not require opening the meeting.
