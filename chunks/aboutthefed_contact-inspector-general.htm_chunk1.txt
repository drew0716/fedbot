The OIG conducts audits, investigations, and other reviews relating to the programs and operations of the Board and the CFPB. OIG website OIG Contact page: Contact page OIG Hotline The OIG is also responsible for preventing and detecting fraud and abuse in the programs and operations of the Board and the CFPB. The OIG operates a hotline that serves as a resource for individuals to report suspected fraud, waste, abuse, or mismanagement.
