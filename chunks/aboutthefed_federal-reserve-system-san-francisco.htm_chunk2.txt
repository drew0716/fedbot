Dr. Daly has served on the advisory boards of the Congressional Budget Office, the Social Security Administration, the Office of Rehabilitation Research and Training, the Institute of Medicine, and the Library of Congress. Dr. Daly is a native of Ballwin, Missouri. She earned a bachelor’s degree from the University of Missouri-Kansas City, a master’s degree from the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, and a PhD from Syracuse University. She also completed a National Institute of Aging post-doctoral fellowship at Northwestern University. She is married and resides in the San Francisco Bay Area.
