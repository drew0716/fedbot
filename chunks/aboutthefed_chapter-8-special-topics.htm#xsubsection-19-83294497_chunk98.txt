Depository institutions assigned a composite CAMELS or CAMELS rating of 4 or 5 or depository institutions that are less than adequately capitalized are not eligible for primary credit. At the discretion of the Reserve Bank, other depository institutions may also be deemed ineligible for primary credit because of supplementary information regarding their financial condition. Return to text 8. Seasonal credit may be extended to depository institutions that are assigned a CAMELS or CAMEL rating of 1, 2, 3, or 4 and are at least adequately capitalized. At the discretion of the Reserve Bank, however, depository institutions that otherwise meet these criteria may be deemed ineligible for seasonal credit because of supplementary information regarding their financial condition.
