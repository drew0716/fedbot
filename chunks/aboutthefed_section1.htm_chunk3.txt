Short title Be it enacted by the Senate and House of Representatives of the United States of America in Congress assembled , That the short title of this Act shall be the "Federal Reserve Act." [12 USC 226. Part of original Federal Reserve Act; not amended.] 2. Definition of "bank" Wherever the word "bank" is used in this Act, the word shall be held to include State bank, banking association, and trust company, except where national banks or Federal reserve banks are specifically referred to. For purposes of this Act, a State bank includes any bank which is operating under the Code of Law for the District of Columbia. [12 USC 221. Part of original Federal Reserve Act.
