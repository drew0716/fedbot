Closed Board Meeting on January 15, 2021 Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice Notice of a Portion of a Meeting under Expedited Procedures On Friday, January 15, 2021 at 10:00 a.m. , a portion of the closed meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under expedited procedures, as set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding Public Observation of Meetings, via conference call, to consider the following matters of official Board business. Meeting Date: Friday, January 15, 2021 Matter(s) Considered: 1. Periodic Briefing and Discussion on Financial Markets, Institutions, and Infrastructure. 9(A)(i) Effective January 15, 2021 , the meeting was closed to public observation by Order of the Board of Governors 1 because the matters fall under exemption(s) 9(A)(i) of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. Section 552b(c)), and it was determined that the public interest did not require opening the meeting. For more information please contact: Michelle Smith, Director, Assistant to the Board, Division of Board Members at 202-452-2955. Supplementary Information:
