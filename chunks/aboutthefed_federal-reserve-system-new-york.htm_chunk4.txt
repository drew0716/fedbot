Website: https://www.newyorkfed.org/ Branches The Federal Reserve officially identifies Districts by number and Reserve Bank city. The System serves commonwealths and territories as follows: the New York Bank serves the Commonwealth of Puerto Rico and the U.S. Virgin Islands; the San Francisco Bank serves American Samoa, Guam, and the Commonwealth of the Northern Mariana Islands. In the 12th District, the Seattle Branch serves Alaska, and the San Francisco Bank serves Hawaii. The Board of Governors revised the branch boundaries of the System in February 1996.
