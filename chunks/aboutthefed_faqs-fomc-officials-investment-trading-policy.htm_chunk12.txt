Implementation of the Policy for Covered Individuals Q1: When is the effective date of the revised policy? Updated: 2/5/2024 A1: The effective date of the revised policy is January 30, 2024, with the exception that the new covered individual category (8) of section (I) of the FOMC Policy on Investment and Trading for Committee Participants and Federal Reserve System Staff will be effective June 30, 2024. Q2: Why was the prior policy ("Investment and Trading Policy for FOMC Officials") revised? Updated: 2/5/2024 A2: In April 2023, the Board’s Office of Inspector General (OIG) published recommendations stemming from its evaluation of the FOMC’s investment and trading rules and the System’s approach to monitoring employees’ investment and trading activities.
