(e) Rules Relating To Banks With Financial Subsidiaries. Financial Subsidiary Defined. For purposes of this section and section 23B, the term "financial subsidiary" means any company that is a subsidiary of a bank that would be a financial subsidiary of a national bank under section 5136A of the Revised Statutes of the United States. Financial Subsidiary Treated As An Affiliate. For purposes of applying this section and section 23B, and notwithstanding subsection (b)(2) of this section or section 23B(d)(1), a financial subsidiary of a bank-- shall be deemed to be an affiliate of the bank; and shall not be deemed to be a subsidiary of the bank.
