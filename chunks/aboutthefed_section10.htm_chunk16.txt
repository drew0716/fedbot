Office of the Comptroller of the Currency Section three hundred and twenty-four of the Revised Statutes of the United States shall be amended so as to read as follows: (a) Office Of The Comptroller Of The Currency Established. There is established in the Department of the Treasury a bureau to be known as the "Office of the Comptroller of the Currency" which is charged with assuring the safety and soundness of, and compliance with laws and regulations, fair access to financial services, and fair treatment of customers by, the institutions and other persons subject to its jurisdiction.
