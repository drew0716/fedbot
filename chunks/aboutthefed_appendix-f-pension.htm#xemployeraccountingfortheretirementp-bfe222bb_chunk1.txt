Pension F.1 Employer Accounting for the Retirement Plan for the Employees of the Federal Reserve System and the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau The purpose of this memorandum is to document the considerations and conclusions relevant to determining how the Federal Reserve's financial statements should reflect the employer accounting for the Retirement Plan for Employees of the Federal Reserve System (System Plan).
