She joined the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago in 2001 as an economist after completing a postdoctoral fellowship at Princeton University and serving as an assistant professor of finance at Northwestern University’s Kellogg School of Management. Dr. Paulson’s work has been published in leading academic journals such as the Journal of Political Economy , Review of Economics and Statistics , and Review of Financial Studies . She has served on the board of the Western Economic Association International and the Committee on the Status of Women in the Economics Profession. Dr. Paulson received a BA from Carleton College and a PhD in economics from the University of Chicago.
