(8) Income and Expense (A) Loans Interest income on primary, secondary, and seasonal credit is accrued using the applicable rate established at least every 14 days by the Reserve Banks' boards of directors, subject to review and determination by the Board of Governors. For the three months ended March 31, 2017 and 2016, primary, secondary, and seasonal credit average daily balances were $15 million and $40 million, respectively, and average interest rates were 1.16 percent and 0.82 percent, respectively.
