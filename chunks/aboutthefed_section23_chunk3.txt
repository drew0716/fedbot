For purposes of this section, the term "insured depository institution" has the same meaning as in section 3 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act. [12 USC 371b-2(d). As added by act of Dec. 19, 1991 (105 Stat. 2362).] (e) Rulemaking Authority; Enforcement. The Board may issue such regulations and orders, including definitions consistent with this section, as may be necessary to administer and carry out the purpose of this section. The appropriate Federal banking agency shall enforce compliance with those regulations under section 8 of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act. [12 USC 371b-2(e).
