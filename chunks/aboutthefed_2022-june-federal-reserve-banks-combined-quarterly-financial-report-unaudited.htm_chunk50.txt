Net income and losses from operations of the consolidated VIEs are reported as "Consolidated variable interest entities: Income, net" in the Combined statements of operations. The portion of consolidated VIE net income and loss that is allocated to the non-controlling interests is reported as "Consolidated variable entities: Non-controlling interest (income), net" in the Combined statements of operations.
