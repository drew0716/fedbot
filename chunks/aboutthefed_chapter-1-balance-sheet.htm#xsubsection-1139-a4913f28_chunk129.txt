At the close of business each December 31, this account balance should include only the low-value and infrequent Markets related transactions. 11.99 Central Bank Liquidity Swap Accounts (242-100) When the FRBNY initiates a swap agreement with a foreign central bank for the purpose of obtaining foreign currency, the resulting liability is recorded in this account. For example, the FRBNY may enter into a foreign currency liquidity arrangement whereby the Bank would obtain a foreign currency from a foreign central bank; in this example the foreign currency obtained from the foreign central bank would be recorded as a liability in this account.
