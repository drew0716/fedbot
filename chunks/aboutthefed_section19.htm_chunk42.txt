Section 8(h) of the Federal Deposit Insurance Act shall apply to any proceeding under this subsection. Disbursement. All penalties collected under authority of this subsection shall be deposited into the Treasury. Violate Defined. For purposes of this section, the term "violate" includes any action (alone or with another or others) for or toward causing, bringing about, participating in, counseling, or aiding or abetting a violation. Regulations. The Board shall prescribe regulations establishing such procedures as may be necessary to carry out this subsection.
