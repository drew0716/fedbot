Liquidity Facilities The Federal Reserve is using its full range of tools to support the flow of credit to households and businesses. Pursuant to section 13(3) of the Federal Reserve Act (FRA) and after obtaining the requisite approval from the Secretary of the Treasury, the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve established facilities with broad-based eligibility. The Board of Governors established three lending facilities (note 2) and formed five limited liability companies (LLCs, note 4) for additional lending programs to provide liquidity to various sectors of the economy.
