FAQs The following FAQs provide clarification and guidance on the Investment and Trading Policies for Covered Individuals ("the Policy for Covered Individuals"), which is section (V) of the FOMC Policy on Investment and Trading for Committee Participants and Federal Reserve System Staff . Section (V) revises and renames the Investment and Trading Policy for FOMC Officials and sets forth the rules that govern investment and trading activities, as well as disclosure, for covered individuals. Please contact Board or Reserve Bank ethics staff with questions about the Policy for Covered Individuals or these FAQs.
