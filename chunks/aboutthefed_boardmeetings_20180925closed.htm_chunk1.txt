Closed Board Meeting on September 25-26, 2018 Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice Notice of a Meeting under Expedited Procedures On Tuesday, September 25, 2018 at 2:00 p.m. and continued on Wednesday, September 26, 2018 at 9:00 a.m. , a meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under expedited procedures, as set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding Public Observation of Meetings, at the Board's offices at 20th Street and C Streets, N.W., Washington, D.C., to consider the following matters of official Board business. Meeting Date: Tuesday, September 25, 2018 Matter(s) Considered: 1. Discussion of Monetary Policy Issues. 9(A)(i) Effective September 25, 2018 , the meeting was closed to public observation by Order of the Board of Governors 1 because the matters fall under exemption(s) 9(A)(i) of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. Section 552b(c)), and it was determined that the public interest did not require opening the meeting. For more information please contact: Michelle Smith, Director, or Dave Skidmore, Assistant
