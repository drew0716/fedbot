Closed Board Meeting on November 2-3, 2021 Government in the Sunshine Meeting Notice Notice of a Meeting under Expedited Procedures On Tuesday, November 2, 2021 at 1:00 p.m. and continued on Wednesday, November 3, 2021 at 9:00 a.m. , a meeting of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System was held under expedited procedures, as set forth in section 261b.7 of the Board's Rules Regarding Public Observation of Meetings, via audio/video conference call, to consider the following matters of official Board business. Meeting Date: Tuesday, November 2, 2021 Matter(s) Considered: 1. Discussion of Monetary Policy Issues. 9(A)(i) Effective November 2, 2021 , the meeting was closed to public observation by Order of the Board of Governors 1 because the matters fall under exemption(s) 9(A)(i) of the Government in the Sunshine Act (5 U.S.C. Section 552b(c)), and it was determined that the public interest did not require opening the meeting.
