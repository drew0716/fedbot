Civil Money Penalty Section 30. Saving clause Section 31. Reservation of right to amend Section 23. Interbank Liabilities* (a) Purpose. The purpose of this section is to limit the risks that the failure of a large depository institution (whether or not that institution is an insured depository institution) would pose to insured depository institutions. (b) Aggregate Limits On Insured Depository Institutions' Exposure To Other Depository Institutions. The Board shall, by regulation or order, prescribe standards that have the effect of limiting the risks posed by an insured depository institution's exposure to any other depository institution.
